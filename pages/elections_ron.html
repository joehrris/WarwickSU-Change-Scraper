Re-open Nominations

Re-open nominations are for any student who doesn't feel that the current nominees for the specific position should be elected in and voted upon. If this position wins any election, then there will be a subsequent by-election conducted in line with our rules and regulations agreed upon by the Deputy Returning Officer.

Contact Us

If you need to ask any questions about the elections process, you can visit us in the Membership Office on the top floor of SUHQ. Alternatively, you can contact us using the details below.
