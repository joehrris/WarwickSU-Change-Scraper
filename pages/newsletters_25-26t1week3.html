Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,

The skies may have turned grey again, but we’ll try to be your little ray of sunshine this week! 🌤️
We’ve got some great things happening on campus – from celebrating our Autumn Elections results, to joining the vibrant Diwali celebrations on Tuesday ( check out the SU event here ), and meeting the incredible Karé Adenegan, Warwick alumna and 7x Paralympic medallist, on Wednesday at the Piazza.
Wishing you all a bright and inspiring week ahead!
Best,
Alijah Taha
President
