Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,

Welcome to Week 2! I hope you’re settling in well and finding your rhythm. I’ve got something exciting lined up that’ll be launching next week – so keep an eye out for my Week 3 email!

For now, please see the updates below, and don’t forget to vote in the Autumn Elections – every vote truly counts in shaping your Students’ Union.

Make Your Mark and cast your vote!

It's time to vote in the Autumn Elections! Choose your next Forum Members, Faculty Reps, Course Reps and Part-Time Student Officers. Vote closes this Friday at 12pm Noon!

The (Free) Breakfast Club is back!

Join us every Tuesday from 8:30am-10:30am in The Green Room! Help yourself to a variety of hot and cold breakfast options. Gluten free and vegan options are also available, including Oatly milk. Remember, once it’s gone, it’s gone - so be sure to get in quick!

Download SafeZone -your link to campus safety and wellbeing

Whether you’re working late, walking alone, or need urgent assistance, SafeZone connects you directly to campus safety and support teams in real time. Find out how SafeZone can support you here.

Diwali - A Celebration of Unity and Culture

Join us on Tuesday 21st October to celebrate Diwali at Warwick! There'll be live DJ sets, creative hands-on workshops like 'Paint Your Own Diya' and 'Henna Art', and you can enjoy delicious Indian food and drink on the Piazza and in Curiositea, Food Station and the Dirty Duck!
