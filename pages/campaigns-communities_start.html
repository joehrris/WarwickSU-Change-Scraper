Launch Your Campaign

Warwick students have a long-standing history of campaigning and activism. Whether it's on campus, locally, or (inter)nationally, every student has the ability to make a difference on issues that matter.

What's more, the SU is here to help! If you'd like support to plan and launch your campaign, simply go to Make A Change

We have a number of funding opportunities to support you - please visit the Fund Your Idea page

If you are part of a club or society, you can also pop into the Membership Office in SUHQ  on the 2nd Floor and speak with one of our coodinators.

You may want to download the campaigns planning sheet to help you plan your campaign.
