Advice Centre

The Advice Centre is a free, professional, confidential, non-judgmental service, independent from the University. If you are experiencing a problem in relation to your studies, accommodation, money or personal problems and need our help, please head to our contacts page to log an enquiry or book an in-person appointment.

Click here to access the Help and Support page

Safeguarding

If you have a concern about abuse of a young person (under 18) or an adult at risk (18 or over with care and support needs) please use our online form to make us aware. Once the form has been submitted, it will be routed to the Warwick Students' Union Safeguarding Leads, who will investigate the matter.

Click here to access the Incident Report Form

Student Activities

To discuss any concerns you have that are associated with the activities of Sports Clubs or Societies, please pop in to the Membership office on the 2nd Floor of SUHQ or email studentactivities@warwicksu.com
