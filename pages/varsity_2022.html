It's competition time!

Here's your chance to WIN 2 x TERM 3 POP! PASSES!

That’s right, you could win entry to every POP! of Term 3 for you and a friend.

How to enter:

  1. Make sure you're following Warwick SU on Instagram

  2. Get yourself down to any of the Varsity weekend games! (click on the Fixtures tab above to see the games)

  3. Post an Instagram story from the game and tag @WarwickSUOfficial so we can share

Good luck!

T&Cs:

  • Competition is live throughout Varsity weekend (25th - 27th February)

  • POP! Passes are non-refundable or transferable

  • The winner will be contacted on the week commencing 28th February via Instagram direct message. A new winner will be contacted if we receive no response within 48 hours.

  • The winner will be asked to email the SU with their name, university card number and proof of winning, to claim their POP! passes
