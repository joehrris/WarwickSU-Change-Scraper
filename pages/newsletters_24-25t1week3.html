Hey y'all,
It's week 3 ALREADY! It's ok, I'm ok (and I hope you're ok!). It's time to find out who’s representing you over the next academic year by heading to our website for the Autumn Elections results :)
If you'd like to make your case for change at the SU , make sure you've submitted your motions for the All Student Vote by the 21st of October (next Monday)! We've also got some events lined up for Black History Month next week - have a look below.
Make sure to take a break and explore some of our gorgeous green spaces 🌲. You can also have your artwork, inspired by the wildlife on campus, displayed in the FAB Agora by submitting your work from today till the 2nd of December! Join the Sustainability at Warwick newsletter to find out more.
Ending this week's newsletter with a fun event to look forward to - our Postgraduate Pub Quiz takes place tonight! We'll see you there :)
Signing off (not really),
Enaya Nihal
SU President

Autumn Elections: Results Available

Results for the Autumn Elections are now available! Find out who your next Course Reps, Faculty Reps and other Part-Time Officers are here.

Submit your motions for the All Student Vote

The All Student Vote (ASV) is an opportunity for Warwick students to debate and vote on motions submitted by fellow students. If you're interested in submitting a motion, you can find out more here. You have until the 21st October to submit!

Postgraduate Pub Quiz

The Postgrad Pub Quiz is back on tonight! Round up your mates and head to The Graduate for 7pm for a fun-filled Monday evening. It's also free entry for teams!

Black History Month: T-shirt making workshop

On Tuesday 22nd October, you're invited to a special T-shirt designing workshop for Black History Month. The workshop offers a creative space to reflect on the power of storytelling and representation, while designing unique T-shirts that symbolise pride and resilience.

That Friday Feeling!

Are you looking for either a chilled out, competitive, or party-filled Friday night? You can enjoy all of these vibes in The Atrium! Whatever Friday Feeling you have, bring that feeling to the SU, the home of Friday nights.

Juicy

Join us every Friday, in the newly refurbished Terrace Bar, for a night brimming with global beats and urban vibes whilst savouring the delights of our new range of Juicy cocktails. Dance the night away to banger after banger from DJ Spincycle, mixing tracks from across the globe.

Wild Warwick

Wild Warwick is a project to promote and celebrate wildlife and green spaces on campus! This competition welcomes submissions of art (photography, paintings, poetry and more) which use the University’s wildlife and green spaces as inspiration. Find out how to enter here.

Sustainability at Warwick newsletter

The October edition of Sustainability at Warwick's newsletter has dropped! This month they're encouraging you to sign up to Climate Literacy Training, recapping their Welcome Week events and inviting everyone to learn about solar panels on campus.
