Your Weekly Newsletter
Hi everyone,
Last week reminded me of something important: student leadership isn’t always neat, quiet, or easy, but it does matter. A lot.
The people who step into those roles are just students who once thought “maybe I could do that.”
If you’ve ever been curious about running in the Spring Officer Elections, take a moment to read the Student Officer testimonies from alumni below. They’re honest, reassuring, and a good reminder that nobody starts off as an expert, but you grow into it.
You’ll also find updates on the Gender Expression Fund, which is still open and supporting students with gender-affirming items, and a snapshot of what your officers achieved in Term 1.
Have a read, see what’s happening this week, and if you’ve ever thought about putting yourself forward, this might be your sign.
Alijah Taha
President
