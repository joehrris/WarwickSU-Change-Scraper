Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,

Hope you’re settling into the week well.

This week is Green Week and it’s a great chance to engage with sustainability in a way that feels accessible and relevant.

The Warwick Sustainability Forum are hosting a range of events and working closely with lots of societies across campus.

A big thank you to our Environment and Ethics Officer, Helena Ratcliffe, and all the brilliant students and volunteers working together to make this week happen.

Have a look at our Instagram to see what’s on and get involved in whatever way works for you.

On a very different note, we’re also showing Super Bowl LX live in the Dirty Duck this Sunday. Patriots vs Seahawks, big screens and a good atmosphere if you fancy ending the week with something fun.

You’ll find everything else happening this week below.

Yours truly,

Alijah Taha

President

Warwick Students’ Union

Watch Super Bowl LX LIVE at the Dirty Duck!

Watch the epic showdown between the New England Patriots and the Seattle Seahawks LIVE this Sunday at the Dirty Duck from 9pm! With great food and drink deals on offer, join us for full coverage throughout the night, including the epic half-time show!

Learn More

Coming Soon: Sexual Health & Guidance Festival

Get ready for SHaG Fest – the SU's exciting annual celebration of sexual health, body positivity and healthy relationships! From advice from sexual health experts, free condoms and lube through to demonstrations, don’t miss out on this energetic, informative, and inclusive event! Sponsored by Sagami.

Learn More

Menstrual Health Day with Lunaire on Thursday 12th February

This event is a dedicated day for students who menstruate to access free, expert-led information on menstrual and reproductive health. It aims to create an open, supportive space to learn more about common and lesser-known conditions, better understand symptoms, and feel empowered to seek help when needed.

Learn More

Sexual Health Drop-in clinic

With BHA for Equality, we'll be setting up an STI testing area in the SU Advice Centre on Wednesday 11th February to access FREE professional asymptomatic testing, advice & condoms. If you're worried, need to know more, or just want to double check and be safe, this is for you!

Learn More

Community Iftar

The Islamic Society will be hosting their Community Iftar in the Islamic Prayer Hall this Thursday. Iftar is the meal Muslims eat when breaking their fast after sunset. Come along and understand more about their practices, get to know one another, and enjoy some free food!
