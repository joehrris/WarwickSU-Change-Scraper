Hiya everyone, and welcome back to another not-so-sunny Term 1 with Warwick SU!
I'm Anna, your SU President (read about your Full-Time Officers below!) and I'll be keeping you up to date with all things WSU throughout the year.
Across October it's Black History Month, focusing on 'Saluting Our Sisters' and the #WEMATTER movement - so keep an eye on our socials to see how we're celebrating it at the SU.
We've also got a packed week ahead, kicking off with a pub quiz for all of our lovely postgrads at the Graduate at 8:30PM TONIGHT and our Autumn elections running all through this week. Nominations close 12pm this Friday, so sign-up and put yourself forward while you can!
If you want something to look forward to... why don't you come and try a burrito at the Food Station, or buy some fresh fruit and veg at Parj's Fruit and Veg Stall in the Atrium next Tuesday?
Otherwise, join us for the first POP! of the year on Wednesday - tickets and POP! passes are on sale now on the SU website. For info about all of this and more, have a sneaky peek below.
'Til we meet (or email) again, folks - have a fab week!
Anna Taylor
SU President
Autumn Elections: Nominate Now!
Nominations are still open for our Autumn Elections! Nominate yourself or a friend now, and have a say in how your SU is run. Closes at 12pm Noon on Friday 6th October.
Get Your Term 1 Tickets and POP! Pass!
Go! Go! Go! Term 1 events tickets are on sale NOW! Be quick, they're sure to sell out soon! You can also grab your POP! Pass, guaranteeing you entry to every single POP! this term.
Meet Our Full-Time Officers!
Each year, the student body elects 7 Full-Time Student Officers to lead the Students' Union. These Officers work alongside our dedicated staff team to deliver a wealth of services for you. Find out more about them here.
Introducing… The Food Station: Your Flavour Destination!
The Bread Oven has been treated to a refresh, and we’re thrilled to unveil The Food Station! With a brand-new interior, additional food choices including baguettes, burritos and an array of grab and go vegan snack selections, we’re sure this will be your first stop for lunch on campus.
Black History Month 2023: 'Saluting Our Sisters’ and the #WEMATTER Movement
Black History Month is a momentous occasion to recognise and celebrate the invaluable contributions of black people from the African Diaspora to British society. Our officers have amazing plans to honour the achievements of black women who are often the forgotten heroines. Visit the Black History Month website here.
Housing FAQs
Your housing journey as a student can be overwhelming and can often seem complicated. We have put together some frequently asked questions, which you can view here. To speak with an Advisor, please submit an enquiry form.
Your New SU Fruit and Veg Stall is Coming Soon!
Every Tuesday in The Atrium from the 10th October, you can say hello to Parj from Maddys Fruit and Veg and treat yourself to lovely, fresh and organic fruit and vegetables, for student-friendly prices!
Postgraduate Pub Quiz
Postgrads, this one's for you! Join us in The Graduate tonight from 8:30pm and rack your brains to win some cash. £1 entry, and winner takes home the cash pot at the end of the night!
