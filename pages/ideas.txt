Make a Change
Transform the SU and University with your brilliant ideas!
The Make a Change platform is the space to shape the future of your Students Union and University experience, offering a simplified way to suggest meaningful changes and act on the issues that matter most to you. Whether you’ve got a simple idea, need a quick fix or have an innovative vision for long term change, this is where it starts.
Why get involved?
Because your voice has power. The platform is built to be easy, accessible, and impactful. You don’t need to be an expert or have all the answers. Just bring your passion, your perspective, and your ideas. Whether it’s about your academic life, wellbeing, clubs or societies, democracy or liberation – it could be anything from a simple idea to a policy proposal to a motion to change existing Bye-laws - we want to hear from you! Get in touch to see how you can make a difference!
