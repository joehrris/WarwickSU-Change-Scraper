Varsity 2023

Varsity is back! Our annual showdown with Coventry SU returns on Saturday 25th of February. Be there and be part of it! We are all #TeamWarwick.

Liberation Conference 2023

Liberation Conference 2023 kicks off this week! Their workshops and lectures are aimed to create thought provoking discussions, and offer a space to learn, talk, interact and discuss openly a range of different topics.

Voting Opens Soon!

Voting opens at 9am on Monday 27th February! Take a look at the candidate list to find out who could be representing your Student Union.

Spring Officer Elections: Question Time

Candidate Question Time is your opportunity to find out more about your 2023 Officer candidates! Two evenings consisting of questions to the candidates and closing speeches. Open to all students, and both events will be live-streamed on our YouTube.

How to Use our Zero Waste Shop

Not sure where to start with our Zero Waste Shop? Watch our handy guide on Instagram, which will help you in three simple steps!

Foodbank

Organised by your Vice President Welfare and Campaigns (Tomi Amole) and your Part Time Officer for Widening Participation (Kieran Barry). Providing a variety of cupboard goods, such as dried pasta, rice, cereal, tinned vegetables and fruit, and similar.

Loud and Proud

DJ Craig Law of Gaydio fame (the world's biggest LGBTQ+ radio station) will be selecting some incredible queer anthems for a fantastic night where we can all be loud and proud! Tuesday 21st of February from 10pm in The Copper Rooms. £3 Entry.

Reflektor

The best indie night in Warwick is back on Saturday 25th of February! Full of new and classic Indie, pop punk, rock, metal, party tunes and guilty pleasures. From 10pm in The Copper Rooms. £6 Entry.
