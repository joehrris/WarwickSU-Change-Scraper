Skool Dayz this Saturday

It's the first Skool Dayz of the term! Don't miss out on our exclusive retro party night in the Copper Rooms! Entry only £6.50. Grab your tickets here!

FTO Wellbeing Corner: Free Menstrual Care Products

We believe that the cost of menstrual care products is an unfair burden placed on those that need them. We are supplying over 30 bathrooms across campus with period pads and tampons, as well as reusable products available upon request. Find your nearest location here.

Wild Warwick Exhibition & Events

The Wild Warwick exhibition is taking place in the FAB Agora space until 24th January. The exhibition includes a great variety of photos, poetry and paintings created by staff and students, using the University's wildlife as inspiration.

Euphoria

Warwick Pride are back with Euphoria this Friday in the Copper Rooms! Come in at 10pm and leave euphoric at 2am, with tickets HOT-TO-GO for £5 for members, £5.50 for non members. You can also snap a polaroid photo for £1.50! Grab your tickets here.

Save the Date: Refreshers Fair
