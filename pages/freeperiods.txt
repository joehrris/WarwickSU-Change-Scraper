The SU believes that the cost of menstrual care products is an unfair burden placed on those that need them and has committed to providing free period products through the #EndPeriodPoverty scheme. We are supplying over 30 bathrooms across campus with Here We Flo pads and tampons, which can also be picked up from the SU reception or ordered discreetly through Locker 21. Find products in the SU buildings, Rootes, Warwick Arts Centre, Chaplaincy, Oculus, Faculty of Arts Warwick Business School and the Library. We also host talks, give out freebies and run collaborations, so please get in touch if you are interested in collaborating on an event with your department, club or society!
For any further enquiries, please contact Naomi Carter (Vice-President Welfare and Campaigns) at
naomi.carter@warwicksu.com
Thank you!
Guidance for University Departments
We have created a set of guidelines for University Departments looking to promote Menstrual Equity in their departments. This includes information on how to order products through the SU (including suppliers and costings), guidance on providing reasonable adjustments for menstrual conditions, and a checklist for the department.

Our supplier and materials
We have partnered with organic period product provider
Here We Flo
to provide the pads and tampons you see on campus. HereWeFlo is a Certified B-Corporation that aims to empower people who have periods to feel more confident, through providing organic and sustainable period products. You can find out more about their mission here and the materials used
here.
Accessing Reusable Products
Though we have carefully chosen the most sustainable provider for our pads and tampons, we recognise that reusable period products can be a more cost-effective, comfortable and environmentally-friendly option. We have a limited stock of reusable menstrual cups from
FloCup
that will soon be available to request online and collect from the Student’s Union.
For other reusable options, we suggest looking at: DAME, MoonCup, Fluxies, ModiBodi or Here We Flo!
Discreet Requests via Locker 21
Locker 21 provides a discreet way of accessing free sexual health and menstrual care products. You can fill out
this form
to request products online and collect from the SU Headquarters - they will be delivered anonymously to Locker 21, on the ground floor of SU Headquarters. Available products include tampons, pads, condoms, lube and dental dams.
