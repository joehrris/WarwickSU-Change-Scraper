Welcome back to Warwick SU!
Merry New Year! Hope you've found some peace over the much-needed winter break and you're ready for another term of procrastination. This is when the real deadlines kick in, unless you're a Social Sciences student... In your case, I'll wait till Term 3 starts to wish you good luck on your all-nighters in the library.
Good news! (Unrelated to Wicked) We're giving away free money! The SU's funding opportunities are now open. If you'd like to Fund Your Idea , use the Gender Expression Fund , or get some Sportswear (for All) , you can apply on our website today.
If you're an International student, listen up !! The Russell Group Students' Union (super exclusive club we're part of) is running a survey aimed to improve your experience- make sure to fill the form out here .
Want your exam timetables released earlier so you can cram earlier? If yes, and if you've got one more survey in you, fill out this form run by your VP Education, Muneeba.
Lastly, if you like to write cool emails like this, you've gotta do it yourself by running in the Spring Officer Elections - nominations open next Monday!
Your SU President,
Enaya Nihal

Nominations for Spring Officer Elections open next Monday

Do you want to improve the student experience at Warwick? You could nominate yourself or another student in the Spring Officer Elections! Take a look at the available roles here.

SU Funding Opportunities

Got an idea? Need support? The SU has various student-led funds which provide necessary financial support to our student community, making everyday life at university that little bit easier! Take a look at the funds we have available here.

Term 2 Tickets on sale

We've got some amazing events lined up for you in Term 2! Skip the FOMO and grab your tickets now on the SU website before they sell out!

Bollynights: Refreshers Rave

The UK’s Biggest Bollywood Night comes back to Warwick this Friday! Expect Dhol drummers, live performances and many more surprises in the Copper Rooms from 10pm! Buy your tickets here.

Weekly Specials at The Food Station

NEW Toasted Sandwich Specials every week! Crispy, gooey, and packed with flavour! This week, try our pork meatballs and spicy tomato sauce loaded with cheese. Available now at The Food Station for just £4.50. New fillings drop every Monday!

The (Free) Breakfast Club

The (Free) Breakfast Club is back in the Green Room every Tuesday from 8:30am-10:30am! You can enjoy a selection of tea, coffee, cereals, fruit, porridge & toast. Gluten free & vegan options are also available, including Oatly!

Co-options for Remaining Roles

There's still time to nominate yourself for the following co-options roles: International Students Officer (EU), Arts Faculty Rep PGR, Social Sciences Faculty Rep PGR & SU Forum roles. Deadline is Friday 10th January at 12pm Noon.

Calling all International Students!

This is YOUR chance to share your experiences and make your voice heard. Take the RGSU survey and help us create recommendations to improve the student experience for international students like YOU. Plus there are 20 x £50 vouchers up for grabs! Find out more here.
