My Module

At Warwick SU, we believe that students are collaborators and partners in education. This means that not only should students have a say in what and how they are taught, but also that the insight students can provide into the academic experience should be given a public platform.

That’s why we’ve developed two different online module tools for Warwick students to use – Liberate My Module and Discover My Module.

Share your thoughts on how well modules you’ve taken have embedded issues of equality, diversity and liberation within the curriculum and learning environment.
