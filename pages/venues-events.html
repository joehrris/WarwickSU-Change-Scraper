Food, Drink & Events

Warwick SU owns and runs its very own group of commercial outlets ( The Food Station , Curiositea , The Dirty Duck and The Terrace Bar ) which provide some of the best-value food and drink anywhere on campus. We also have a great space for studying in The Green Room located in the SU main building.

Our nightclub, The Copper Rooms, hosts a variety of fantastic, student-friendly events every week. Discover our regular events here .

All our commercial venues combined employ hundreds of members of student staff!
