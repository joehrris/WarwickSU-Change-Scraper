'Ello dudes and dudettes,

Hope you've had a weekend full of partying (or studying for the poor souls who still have exams on)! It's finally week 8, which means that Sports Ball is on Friday!! If you were lucky enough to cop a ticket, I'll see you there ;) 🏀🎾🪩

Tuesday will see our very first Liberation Assembly take place in the Kevin Gately room, come along at 4:30pm 🗽
