Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,
This week’s a good one to pause and speak up because the Warwick Student Survey is open, and it’s genuinely one of the best ways to shape what comes next for all of us here. It only takes a few minutes, and it really does make a difference (plus, there’s a chance to win vouchers too).
We’ve also got Islamophobia Awareness Month happening and Thrift Thursday in the Atrium, all the details are just below.
Best,
Alijah Taha
President
Warwick Students’ Union

Islamophobia Awareness Month

November is Islamophobia Awareness Month, which aims to educate people on what Islamophobia is and how it manifests within our society and wider culture. It also aims to educate people on the culture and lived experiences of Muslims to break down barriers and reduce prejudice.

Thrift Thursday

Kickstart your sustainable fashion era in the Atrium this Thursday from 10am! High street samples, vintage treasures and handmade goods - you'll find them all at Thrift Thursday! BYOTB (Bring Your Own Tote Bag!)

Learn More

Your voice makes waves!

Offer your feedback this term in the Warwick Student Survey so that we can work together to keep improving your experiences. Plus, you could win shopping vouchers! The survey will include questions about your connections, wellbeing, support, key SU services, and using AI in your studies.

Free period products across campus

If you need menstrual products, we are supplying over 30 bathrooms across campus with pads and tampons. Find your closest pick-up point here.
