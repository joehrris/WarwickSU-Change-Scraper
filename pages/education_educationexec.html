Ananya Sreekumar

Vice President for Education

She/Her

As VP Education, I represent students on all academic related matters, I'm the Student-Staff Liaison Committee (SSLC) lead, and I'm the point of contact in the SU for academic matters.

Hometown :
Bengluru, India

Fun fact about me :
I LOVE wildlife! I love going to conservation sites, as well as snapping some wildlife photography!

My role and what I hope to achieve this year :
As VP Education, I hope to improve the education experience for all students, push for accessibility within education, and ensure every student has fair opportunities at Warwick.

  • Build the Decolonise Project this year
  • Look more into students experience with neurodivergence and racism
  • Ensuring all departments have adequate self-certification policies

The Vice President for Education's office is on the first floor of SUHQ.
