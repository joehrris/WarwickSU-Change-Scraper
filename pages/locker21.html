Locker 21 Scheme

Our Locker 21 Scheme offers a way for students to discreetly request safer sex and menstural products by simply filling in the request form below. Your request will remain completely confidential.

You can use this form to request condoms, dental dams, lube, pregnancy tests, towels, and tampons. Once requested, your items will be ready for collection within 24 hours from Locker 21 , which is located on the ground floor of SUHQ, near the lift. No key will be required.

You can also drop by SU HQ Welcome Hub or email the Welfare Officer via welfare@warwicksu.com !

Please note however supplies are limited and that this service is for exceptional circumstances, and should not be relied upon for all your needs.
