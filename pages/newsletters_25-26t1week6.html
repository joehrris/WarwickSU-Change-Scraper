Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,

Couple of quick things this week:

If you haven’t done the Warwick Student Survey yet, take a few minutes and get it done. It’s one of those small things that actually ends up making a difference.

Our American Football team Warwick Wolves have a big game coming up against Nottingham Trent on 22nd November, and our cheerleading squad Warwick Devils will be performing at halftime, so always worth watching. Tickets are up if you fancy going!

Also, have a look at Celebrating Academic Integrity: there are a few events and even a comp you can jump into if that’s your thing.

See you around,

Alijah Taha

President

Disability Item Fund

The Disability Item Fund is now open! This fund is designed to support disabled students by covering the cost of small or necessary essential items or services (up to £50 per person) that improve access, wellbeing, or participation in student life.
