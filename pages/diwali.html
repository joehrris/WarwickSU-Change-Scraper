Diwali Project – A Celebration of Unity and Culture

Event from Campaigns & Communities

Tue 21 October 2025 11:00-16:00
21/10/2025, 11:00 - 21/10/2025, 16:00

Tuesday 21 October 2025, 11.00AM - Tuesday 21 October 2025, 4.00PM

Piazza, Copper Room 2

Review Basket Proceed
The Students’ Union is excited to present the Diwali Project , a vibrant and inclusive celebration forming part of Warwick University's One World Warwick events programme. This initiative brings together students from diverse backgrounds to celebrate the Festival of Lights, fostering cultural awareness and inclusivity across campus.
The Diwali Project will offer a platform for students to share their cultural experiences, traditions, and values, promoting mutual understanding and respect. Through interactive workshops and collaborative activities, this celebration enhances cross-cultural dialogue and strengthens community bonds within the university.

Join Us on Tuesday, 21 st October for an Amazing Event!

Diwali Celebration (11:00 - 16:00 - Piazza):
Celebrate Diwali and enjoy the festive atmosphere, dance, and connect with students from all around the world on the Piazza with:
  • Live DJ sets
  • Dhol player
  • T-shirt designing
Also enjoy delicious Indian food and drink on the Piazza, Curiositea, Food Station and the Dirty Duck.
Creative Diwali Workshops (11:00 - 16:00 - Copper Room 2):
Get creative with five hands-on workshops :
  • Paint Your Own Diya - Decorate a traditional oil lamp, symbolising light overcoming darkness.
  • Create a Diwali Bookmark - Design a vibrant, intricate bookmark for your favourite book.
  • Make Your Own Diwali Card - Craft personalised cards to share warm Diwali wishes.
  • Mandala Windchimes - Decorate windchimes that symbolise protection, positivity, and manifestation.
  • Henna Art - Adorn your hands with beautiful henna designs, symbolising joy, and prosperity.
Plus, enjoy delicious Indian sweets and tea while you celebrate! Whether you want to dance, create, or learn, this event offers something for everyone, while deepening your understanding of the diverse communities at Warwick.
Let’s light up the campus and celebrate the Festival of Lights together!
