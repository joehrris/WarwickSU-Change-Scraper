Wins get relived, losses get forgotten, and everyone comes together in true Warwick style. Think pop anthems, throwback bangers, guilty pleasures, and a dancefloor that doesn’t calm down.
Varsity week is over, and Warwick is doing what it does best, celebrating properly. Whether you played, supported from the sidelines, or just lived for the atmosphere, this is the night that wraps up the biggest sports week of the year.
This is the night Varsity deserves — loud, unapologetic, and full of the kind of fun that only happens in the Copper Rooms. We’re bringing together another quintessential Warwick sports night. Expect packed circles, team chants, sweaty kits turned party fits, and that unbeatable post-Varsity buzz.
Circling from 8pm
Club night 11pm–2am
Wear black and red kit, bring your team, and come take part in Warwick's first Varsity After Party.
Entry is £5.00, tickets on sale now via the SU Website… with tickets in high demand, be prepared to ensure you do not miss out!
Circling and Event tickets will give access from 8pm - only buy this ticket if you are intending to circle!
Event Only Tickets give access from 11pm
