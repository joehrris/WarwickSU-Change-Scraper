Warwick SU Print Shop is a service and is available for any Warwick University student, club or society. We can help you with all of your print needs. Once your order has been received and confirmed, please allow 5 working days turnaround unless otherwise stated. All orders will need to be picked-up from the Students’ Union HQ Reception.

Please download our pricelist to find out more (full price list also detailed below). All prices include VAT where applicable and are subject to change. Prices stated are available to students at the University of Warwick only. Please contact us for a quote for non-students and commercial projects.

We don't offer a design service, but we can help by pointing you in the right direction of freelancers or agencies for you to contact direct and discuss your requirements. Just ask!

Placing Your Order: Please email print@warwicksu.com with the following details:

  1. Quantity required
  2. Size
  3. Format: landscape / portrait / any folding or stapling required
  4. Single or Double Sided
  5. Colour or Black & White
  6. Any Special Requirements
  7. Please attached your final artwork to the email in a PDF format
  8. Your name and contact details, phone and email

Send us an email to place an order .
