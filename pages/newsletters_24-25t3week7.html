Your Weekly Newsletter

What's cookin', good-lookin'?

I'm back with some cool things to look forward to (and a smidge of a warning about buses RIP)...

Firstly, the (Cruel) Summer Elections have come to a close! Louis will be back as your VP Sports 🤺 Find the full results on our website :) 🪸

The heaviest set of assignments and exams should be ending, so don your themed circle outfits for POP! Thrift Thursdays are also back this week 🛍️

The A429 Road in Kenilworth will be closed from today until the end of July, barring a weekend, so be sure to leave a little earlier in the mornings if you’re a Kenilworth or Leam-dweller ⚠️

And finally, do fill in the Student Feedback Survey!!

See ya next time,

Your SU President,
Enaya 🩷
