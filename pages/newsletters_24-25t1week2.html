Warwick SU week 1 (w/c 2nd October) All Member Email View Online version
Student Voice Societies & Sports Advice Campaigns & Communities Events
Hey there,
You made it through Week 1! I hope you all got a chance to go to POP! or to nab a succulent at the plant sale last week - but fear not, we have a host of fun planned for this week too.
First things first, voting is now OPEN for the Autumn Elections . It's time to choose your next Course Reps , Part-Time Officers , and Forum Reps for this academic year. Head over to our website to make your voice heard!
Also, October is Black History Month ! The national campaign's theme for this year is Reclaim the Narrative - find out what's on below :)
The University is also launching Climate literacy training (which counts for your Warwick Award ), as well as hosting a roadshow in our Atrium building this week, consulting you on their new brand ! While you're there, make sure to drop by the Food Station , where we've got brand new burrito bowls & toasted sandwiches ready for you.
I wake up and it's (already) October! The loss is yours if you've not bought your Skool Dayz ticket for this Saturday or checked out our Party Bus to Players (a very cool karaoke place) in Coventry on Friday .
Lastly, the start of term can be a really stressful time for everyone . I know how tough it can get, juggling lectures, socials, sports, and getting used to Uni life. Our Advice Centre is here for you throughout the year, so please reach out if you need anything :)
See you all next week!
Enaya Nihal
SU President

Make Your Mark in your Autumn Elections!

Voting is now open for the Autumn Elections! Make Your Mark and vote for who you want to represent your views at the SU. Voting closes at 12pm on Friday 11th October.

Black History Month: Reclaim the Narrative

October is Black History Month, with the theme being Reclaim the Narrative. Watch this space to see what events the SU has to celebrate, and click here find out more about Black History month.

Explore Warwick’s proposed brand concepts

The Brand Evolution Project Roadshow is live! Share your views on the proposed concepts and help shape Warwick's identity. Join us online and at key campus locations until 13th October.

SU Advice Centre: We're here to help

Our Advice Centre is a free, professional, independent, and confidential service. We aim to provide you with high quality advice, information, and representation. If you're seeking support, get in touch here, and one of our trusted advisors will be in touch as soon as they can.

Party Bus to Coventry

The Party Bus now takes you to Players Entertainment in Coventry! For just £5, your ticket gets you: Transport to/from Players, Free VK/J2) from Terrace Bar AND a free fishbowl in Players! Don't miss out and secure your place before they sell out!

Skool Dayz

It's the first Skool Dayz of the term! Dance the night away to 00s and 10s pop bangers, hip hop hits, electronic dance classics and more! Grab your tickets here.

Climate Literacy Training

The university are pleased to announce the launch of Climate Literacy Training! This training will provide a fundamental awareness of how our way of living is having an impact on the planet, and how we can make a difference by reducing those impacts. Find out more here.

Warm yourself up at The Food Station

Feeling a little chilly on campus? Warm yourself up with our new Toasted Sandwiches from £4.50, or Burrito Bowls from £5! Open Monday-Friday from 11am-3pm.
