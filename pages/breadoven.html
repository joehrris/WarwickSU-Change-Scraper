Food & Drink

Our Restaurants, Bars & Spaces

Visit the SU’s outlets and venues to enjoy our vibrant student community. Whether it’s a coffee-fuelled study session at Curiositea, a cocktail or mocktail in the Terrace Bar, a bite to eat at The Dirty Duck with friends, or lunch from the Food Station, the SU’s outlets are a great way to make the most of life on campus.

Open Monday-Friday from 9am-6pm and Saturday-Sunday from 11am-5pm

Most of our outlets have lower access points for wheelchair accessiblity. Staff are trained to prioritise customers in these areas. If in doubt, speak to a member of staff and we will endeavour to meet your needs.
