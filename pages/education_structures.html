Representation Structure

The Education Officer doesn't work alone; they also have a team of Faculty Representatives that help coordinate with the SSLCs. Each Rep is responsible for ensuring that the SSLCs in their faculties are meeting at least once per term, collecting reports and minutes, and reporting major issues back to the Education Officer. They’re also a first instance problem solver, so can respond to some student issues or direct them to the appropriate person.
