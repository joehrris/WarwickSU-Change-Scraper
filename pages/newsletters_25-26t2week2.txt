Your Weekly Newsletter
Hi everyone,
Just a quick note before we get into what’s happening this week.
Nominations for the Spring Officer Elections are still open, and I really want to encourage you to consider putting yourself forward.
These roles are not about having all the answers. They are about students who care enough to speak up, represent others, and help shape the experience here.
If you have ever felt unheard or thought things could be better, this is your chance to do something about it.
Below, you’ll find everything else going on this week.
Yours Truly,
Alijah Taha
