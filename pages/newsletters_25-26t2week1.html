Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,
Welcome back to Term 2. I hope you had a good break, even though it already feels like time has flown by.
There’s a lot coming up this term, so here are a few highlights to keep on your radar.
Nominations are now open for the Spring Officer Elections. If you’ve ever thought about leading change, shaping student life, or representing your fellow students, I’d really encourage you to put yourself forward.
You can also come and see us at “SU & You” in the Atrium on Tuesday. I’ll be there and would genuinely love to say hello, hear how your term is starting, and chat about anything that’s on your mind.
Term 2 tickets are now on sale, and we’re also launching our new Eat Well Hub, which replaces The (Free) Breakfast Club and focuses on accessible, nourishing food for students.
Finally, please save the date for our Annual Members Meeting on Tuesday 5 May. It’s an important moment for our Union, and you’re all invited to be part of it.
Wishing you a strong start to the term, and hopefully see you around campus very soon.
With commitment to every student,
Alijah Taha
President
Warwick Students’ Union
