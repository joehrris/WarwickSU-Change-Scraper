Hello everyone,
The results are in, and you have all had your say on your future representatives. Thank you to everyone who voted, it was great to see almost 5,000 voters and 27,266 votes cast! Anna, your current VP of Societies, will be taking over as President in August. Take a look below to see who else you have elected to represent your Students' Union.
Things are starting to get back to normal this week; Green Week begins from today, and we're also hosting a bunch of events for International Women's Week, with International Women's Day commencing on Wednesday. Follow the links to see everything that's going on!
Finally, you may remember taking part in the Student Feedback Survey last term. This is vital feedback that is already being used across the SU and University. You can view the results below.
That’s it for this week! After all the pestering to vote last week, I hope you all have a good one despite entering essay deadline season!
Will Brewer SU
President

Spring Officer Elections: The Results

Your next Student Officers have been elected! Click here to find out who will lead the next chapter of the student experience at Warwick.

International Women's Day

It's International Women's Day this Wednesday, with this year's theme focusing on #EmbraceEquity. We've got some fantastic events in store for you this week, including an exclusive Q&A with Zarah Sultana!

Green Week

It's also Green Week this week! Combining with International Women's Week, we'll be in Curiositea tonight at 6pm to teach you how to create your own sustainable menstrual pad. You can grab a free hot drink too!

SCAM ALERT!

Several Warwick students have recently been conned into making payments over the phone to people pretending to be from HMRC or Immigration/Visa Control. If you receive a call like this, DO NOT make any payments; contact the SU Advice Centre for help.

Zero Waste Shop

Seeing at it's Green Week, why not try using our Zero Waste Shop in the Welcome Hub? We offer a range of healthy snacks, core cereals and rice, soap, conditioner and washing detergent. Grab your containers and pop on down!

Student Feedback Survey Results

Thank you to everyone who took part in the Student Feedback Survey in Term One. The response rate was fantastic, and we now have some really useful information about what you like, and where there is room for improvement at the SU and University.
