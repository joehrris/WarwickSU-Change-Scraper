Warwick SU Welcome Fair 2022 View Online version

Welcome Week is less than a week away, and we are so excited to welcome back our returning students!
We’re kicking off next week with our Welcome Fair, on Monday 26 th and Tuesday 27 th September from 11am – 4pm, held in Warwick SU’s Atrium building .
Come along to meet our student-friendly partners and bag yourself some freebies, from Domino’s pizza, to pick ‘n’ mix, stationery, coffee cups, vouchers and more! You can enter plenty of exciting competitions, sign up to receive job opportunities and even get yourself a bus pass.
The Warwick SU team will be there to greet you, and we have some fantastic prizes to give away at our stand competitions! With plenty on offer, you won’t want to miss out.
See what’s on via our Welcome Week website and don’t forget to follow us on Instagram to keep up to date.
Check out fantastic offers from some of our partners below:

Warwick Arts Centre

We can't wait to welcome you to our state-of-the-art cinemas, gallery, theatre, and concert hall. Plus, as a student, cinema tickets are only £5 when you join our Under 26 Film Club for just £10 per year. Find out more about joining the club!

Cannon Park Shopping Centre

Book secure Season Parking for your car from £3/day at Cannon Park Shopping Centre, your handy one stop shop for everyday shopping. You can now book monthly, quarterly and annual season tickets to save money on your parking during term-time!

Frontline

Frontline is a charity working to make social work better for children. By completing our Frontline programme you’ll become a social worker who helps create positive, sustainable change for children and families. This is the work that makes a difference.

London Review of Books

Get a student subscription to the LRB for just £3.49 a month. Whether you’re studying literature, politics, history or art, the LRB is required reading for any student. Subscribe now and access over 17,000 articles in our archive.

Kasbah Nightclub

Coventry’s largest student nightclub consisting of 3 rooms, 2 floors and a huge beer garden. Weekly themed club nights, celebrity guest appearances, international DJs, drinks from £1.50! Let’s face it… it’s the main reason you came to Warwick!

Stagecoach

Get to University with Stagecoach. Just £110 a term (with a 3 term UniRider) or £125 with an Autumn term UniRider. Buses run up to every 7-8 minutes from Leamington. More info here!

Domino's

We think you deserve something special, and we hope a great deal will do the trick! How about 50% off pizza when you spend £30? All you need to do is use the code STUDY£30 at checkout. You're welcome!

Food & Drink at Warwick SU

Want to grab a bite to eat whilst browsing the Welcome Fair? Visit our fantastic SU outlets! Curiositea, our tea shop, is situated in the Atrium on the ground floor. The Bread Oven is on the first floor, and The Dirty Duck is accessible from the Atrium lift.
