Hi everyone, Welcome to exam szn everyone! FYI - SUHQ is open on weekends so you can work in peace 🌱

In other news, our ✨60th Celebrations✨ kicked off last Friday! It was incredible to hear stories from SU Presidents from the 60s and 70s – available to watch on the SU website soon 🎥

We also launched the 60th Exhibition in the SU Atrium, live from today, featuring gorgeous images taken in 1975 for The Boar that I found while researching for the yearbook, as well as some fun moments from our history.

If you'd like to be a part of the legacy going forward, make sure to nominate yourself in our Summer Elections! 🗳️

Your SU President,
Enaya Nihal
