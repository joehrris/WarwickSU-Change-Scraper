Varsity 2024 Fixtures

Varsity 2024

Varsity is back! Our annual showdown with Coventry SU returns on Saturday 24th February.

18 fixtures, 3 dance/cheerleading performances, 7 locations – 1 WINNER!

Make sure you come along and cheer on #TeamWarwick as we aim to make it a 34th consecutive victory in this year's competition!

Be there and be part of it! We are all #TeamWarwick

In addition to the competitive games, there are some friendly fixtures taking place on the same day:

Women's Cricket Sports Arena, Sports and Wellness Hub 10am
Thai Boxing Combat Room, Sports and Wellness Hub 1.30pm
Lacrosse Cryfield Sports Pitches 2pm

Plus we’ll have some amazing dance and cheerleading performances to add an extra dimension to proceedings!

Warwick Classical Modern Dance Sports Arena, Sports and Wellness Hub 2.45pm
Coventry Dance Sports Arena, Sports and Wellness Hub 2.50pm
Cheerleading (Warwick and Coventry) Sports Arena, Sports and Wellness Hub 5pm
