Don't forget to register to vote!

The deadline to register is midnight on Tuesday 18th June! Deadline for registering to vote by post is Wednesday 19th June at 5pm, and by proxy is Wednesday 26th June at 5pm. Click here to register.

Postgraduate Pub Quiz

Postgrads, tonight is your final Pub Quiz of the year! Head down to The Graduate from 7pm, and make this a quiz to remember! Free entry.

Fresh & Juicy

You can still buy the freshest produce on campus every Monday and Thursday until the end of term, with Fresh & Juicy! Catch them between 9am-5pm in The Atrium.

Euro 2024: England v Denmark - Watch in the Copper Rooms

This Thursday you can enjoy watching the England v Denmark match in Copper Rooms 1! Grab your mates, treat yourself to a cold beverage and get ready for kick off at 5pm. Click here for the match details.

Sports Ball & Awards this Friday

The time has finally come for Sports Ball and Sports Awards! Get ready to celebrate your sporting achievements, and dance the night away in The Hilton Metropole at the NEC. Click here for a reminder about all the details.

Safe Space @ The Lodge

If you're going out in Leamington on Friday evenings, remember that the Safe Space at South Lodge on the Parade is open from 10pm-4pm if you're unsafe, vulnerable or in an uncomfortable situation. Find out more here.
