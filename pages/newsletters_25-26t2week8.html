Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,

We had an exciting weekend with Varsity at the Sports Hub, where we won against Coventry for the 36th year in a row! Look at the results below.

National Liberation Conference tickets are also out and they’re free for all Warwick students, so make sure you grab one. It’s an important space and I’d really encourage you to be there.

Postgrads, don’t forget to register for PG Sports Day by 4th March.

Friday Night Lights will be occurring this Friday at Butts Park with a free shuttle bus, bucket hats for the first 100, and plenty of entertainment happening at half-time.

See you there.

Alijah Taha

President

We're the Varsity Champions!

Postgrads, register for our PG Sports Day!

Sign up for our PG Sports Day, happening on Friday 13th March at the Sports Hub! Deadline is Wednesday 4th March, so don't miss out and get involved!

Get your FREE tickets to the National Liberation Conference!

From the 20th-21st March, join us for two days of workshops and sessions full of unique perspectives on how to forge solidarities through successful campaigns, while conspiring and sharing space with likeminded students from across the nation! Tickets are FREE for Warwick students!

Grab your tickets for Friday Night Lights

Get ready for an action-packed Friday of university rugby! There'll be a FREE shuttle bus to the Butts Park Arena, the first 100 attendees will receive an AKUMA bucket hat, and there'll be various DJ, cheer, classical and modern dance half-time performances! Friday 6th March. Tickets: £6.
