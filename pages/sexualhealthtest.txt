With BHA for Equality, we will be setting up an STI testing area in the SU Advice Centre.
Please come along on the 11th of February to access FREE, professional, asymptomatic testing, advice & condoms.
Professionals will be able to offer you advice, sexual health products and tests on the day. We encourage you to make the most of this opportunity if you're worried, need to know more, or just want to double check and be safe!
There will be signage on the day for directions.
Organised by Ollie Chapman, Vice President for Welfare and Campaigns.
