Hi postgrads,
This week sees the return of the All Student Vote , where you get to have your say on the various issues submitted by your fellow students. Successful motions will become official SU policy for us to campaign on, so make sure you take a few moments to vote online once polls open at 9am on Tuesday!
Don’t forget that you can also read the motions on our website and debate them in person at the ASV Meeting , which takes place tonight from 5pm in the SU Atrium.

Contract consultation

In collaboration with UCU and the SU, the University invites postgraduate students who teach to attend informative sessions in order to discuss the proposed changes from hourly-paid contracts to employment contracts.

All Student Vote

Voting opens in this term’s All Student Vote at 9am Tuesday and closes at Noon on Friday. Read the motions online, have your say at the Meeting in the Atrium tonight from 5pm and don’t forget to vote!

Worried about finding your next home?

Warwick Accommodation has properties available in Leamington and Coventry with no booking fees, deposits or guarantors. Whether you’re struggling to find a suitable house off campus or you’re unsure how to find a group to live with, Warwick Accommodation can help. Come along to Cryfield Village Hall on Wednesday 12th February between 1-3pm to talk to us about our off campus properties.
