About Warwick SU
Warwick Students’ Union is one of the finest and busiest in the country with an average footfall of 55,000 per week through its building.
Over 300 clubs and societies help to make Warwick SU the largest in the UK and with 30,000 high calibre students on this campus site, you are guaranteed a quality captive target audience. Warwick SU is one of the most desirable student and community facilities linked to any UK University. Warwick SU benefits from its central location, right in the heart of campus and next door to SUHQ – the administrative and resources centre for students.
We have multiple effective marketing opportunities available for you to put your message in front of students. If you would like to book any activity, discuss an alternative proposal, or if you have any questions about our marketing opportunities, please don’t hesitate to get in touch.
