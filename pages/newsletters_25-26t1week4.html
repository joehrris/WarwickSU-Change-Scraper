Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,

Here’s a few quick highlights for the week:

• Come to the Copper Rooms to celebrate Black History Month on Tuesday 28th October from 2-4pm and get creative in our T-shirt Designing Workshop.

• Dance the night away at our Halloween Ball on Friday 31st October in the Copper Rooms, from 10pm-2am. Costumes encouraged!

• Buses: got thoughts for Stagecoach? Drop your feedback under our latest Insta post @warwicksuofficial so we can pass it on.

See you around campus!

Best,

Alijah Taha

President
