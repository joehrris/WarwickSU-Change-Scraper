Policies

Every student at Warwick is able to propose ideas and motions on a stance or action they feel their Union and Student Officers should take. These motions are taken through your democratic system and either decided at Student Council or through an All Student Vote. All motions that are voted upon and agreed by students then become active Union policy for 3 years.

Below, you will find all currently active policies that have been passed via Student Council or an All Student Vote. We have split these policies into 7 policy zones that broadly align to the work of your Student Officers and SU Committees. You can read more about Student Council and our democratic system by clicking here .

If you have any questions about any of our policies, please contact your Student Officers or the Democracy Team, who will be happy to help.
