Terms and Conditions for use of the Membership System API
The following terms and conditions are subject to change at any time without notice.
The availability of the API is not guaranteed and may be withdrawn at any time.
As a registered exec of a club, society or organisation you may use the membership API to view a live feed of your members details. Should you want to store these details you must give your members clear and easy access to them and act to remove or update them at your members request.
When validating a members login details and/or person key you must take all care to ensure that this key remains private. You may not ask your members to provide Students' Union login details or keep any record of activity relating to the Students' Union website.
