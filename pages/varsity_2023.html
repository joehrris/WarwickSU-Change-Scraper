Varsity 2023

Varsity is back! Our annual showdown with Coventry SU returns on Saturday 25th.

20 matches, 16 sports, 7 locations – 1 WINNER!

Make sure you come along and cheer on #TeamWarwick as we aim to make it a 33rd consecutive victory in this year's competition!

Be there and be part of it! We are all #TeamWarwick

In addition to the competitive games, there are some friendly matches taking place on the same day:

Women's Cricket inter-club match Zone D 10:30 - 13:00
Men's Basketball inter-club match Zone B 10:00 - 12:00
Women's Basketball inter-club match Zone B 12:00 - 14:00
Men's Rugby League v Leicester University Cryfield 13:00 - 15:00
