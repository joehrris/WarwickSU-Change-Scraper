Varsity 2026
Varsity is back! Our annual showdown with Coventry SU returns, Friday 27th to Saturday 28th February.
Across two action-packed days of sport, Warwick’s finest teams will go head-to-head with Coventry in an epic showdown you won’t want to miss.
The first 2 of the 20 fixtures will be a double-header of Basketball action at the Sports and Wellness Hub, as the Warwick Men’s and Women’s teams look to kickstart a winning weekend.
This will be the place to be on Friday night, and spaces are limited, so get your
free ticket now!
Expect fierce rivalries, last-minute winners, and atmosphere that goes way beyond the sidelines.
