Your Weekly Newsletter
Hi everyone,
Hope you’re all doing okay. Here’s a quick look at what’s happening this week:
Gender Expression Fund: Opens Monday 18 November, 9am
You can claim up to £50 for gender-affirming items on the SU website.
Langar on Campus, Tuesday 18th November, 11am – 3pm in the Atrium
Organised by the Sikh Society. Come along for a free hot meal to celebrate the 556th Gurpurab of Guru Nanak Dev Ji.
Housing Day Wednesday 20 November, 10am – 4pm in the Atrium
A great chance to understand renting, explore your options, and make confident housing choices for next year.
See you around the SU this week!
Alijah Taha
President
