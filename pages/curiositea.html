At the heart of Warwick University campus, our friendly team, cosy setting and relaxed vibe welcomes you to Curiositea.

We offer a wide selection of coffees, hot chocolates and teas to warm your soul alongside an extensive menu of milkshakes, frappes, fruit smoothies and iced drinks.

Our menu boasts 5 different milks to choose from and a vast array of syrups and toppings. From a simple, homely teabag tea to an indulgent deluxe hot chocolate, and weekly specials, you’re sure to find something on our menu that suits you.

We also serve a selection of toasties, paninis and wraps and a rotating selection of cakes, including gluten-free and vegan options to keep your hunger at bay.

As part of Warwick Students Union, our profits are reinvested back into the student body through sports, societies and welfare, amongst a multitude of other student activities and support.

Whether you’re coming alone to study, relaxing with a group of friends or just looking for a quick coffee to takeaway - you're always welcome in Curiositea.

  • Coffees

    We use 100% Arabica beans from Brazil, Honduras and Guatemala. Roasted medium-dark, our coffee packs a punch of stone fruit, cocoa, hazelnut and chocolate fudge in milk .

    Espresso - a simple extracted shot of coffee

    Espresso Macchiato - espresso topped with a small amount of foamed milk

    Americano - espresso poured over hot water. Add milk to your taste.

    Latte - espresso topped with steamed milk

    Cappuccino - espresso with foamed milk

    Flat White - double espresso, served short, with a silky smooth steamed milk

    Mocha - Our classic hot chocolate blended with espresso and steamed milk

  • Hot Chocolate

    Classic - We use a 33% cocoa base for our classic hot chocolate. Completely vegan, just switch up your milk to your preferences or dietary needs.

    Deluxe - our classic hot chocolate topped with cream, marshmallows, caramel sauce and a flake

    White - Creamy white chocolate with steamed milk

    Nutella - Nutella whisked together with steamed milk

    White Mocha -Our white hot chocolate blended with espresso and steamed milk

    Milkshakes and Frappes

    We serve a selection of milkshakes and frappes all year round. Available made with dairy milk or Oatly.


  • Hotties

    Teabag Tea - Yorkshire tea or decaf available

    Matcha Latte - Japanese green tea powder with steamed milk

    Chai Latte - Spiced tea with steamed milk

    Karak Chai - Sweet, strong and spicy tea with evaporated milk

    Loose Leaf Tea - Choose from a selection of herbal, black and green teas

    Smoothies

    Our smoothies are made with 100% fruit, and are completely vegan and free from the top 14 allergens.

    Blue Lagoon peach, mango, pineapple, banana, apple + blue spirulina

    Purple Dragon dragonfruit, apple, strawberry, mango + papaya

    Berry Bliss strawberry, blueberry, raspberry, blackberry + apple

    Tropical Temptation pineapple, apple, papaya + mango
