Your Weekly Newsletter

Hi everyone,

Before you dive into everything below, just a quick hello from me. I hope you enjoyed my very dramatic weather updates during Storm Claudia. Although, I think I’ve actually caught a cold from running around in it. So, my advice: don’t be like Alijah. Stay warm.
Thrift Thursday is back this week, so it’s a good chance to pick up some fun vintage bits before Christmas arrives. If you came to the Global Illuminations event in Week 7, we'd love to hear what you though of it via the feedback form here .

With the 16 Days of Activism Against Gender-Based Violence underway, keep an eye on our Instagram Stories for daily info and resources, join us in Piazza on 5th December, and feel free to fill out the Russell Group SU survey linked in the article below.
If you see me around campus, come say hi.
Alijah Taha
President
Warwick Students’ Union

Thrift Thursday

It's the final Thrift Thursday of the year! Join us in the Atrium from 10am and kickstart your sustainable shopping era, just in time for Christmas!

Warwick Sustainability: Coppicing

Did you know that sometimes cutting down trees can be good for wildlife? Join Warwick Sustainability on Wednesday 3rd December to learn this ancient form of woodland management, help improve the campus habitat and benefit wildlife.

Russell Group SU's Gender-Based Violence Survey

Have you ever felt unsafe on campus? This survey is your chance to speak up and help shape better protections across the Russell Group. You can answer anonymously, and skip any questions you're not comfortable answering.

Gender-Based Violence Solidarity Panel

As part of the 16 Days of Activism Against Gender-Based Violence campaign, we're collaborating with Warwick Anti-Sexism Society to bring you the Gender-Based Violence Solidarity Panel on Friday 5th December from 1pm-3pm. Find out more about the 16 days campaign here.
